Conversion Kits

Many of our customers desire to convert their bikes into e-bikes, making it one of our most popular services. Our motors come in a range of 250W to 1500W. If you're interested in learning more about our conversion kits, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Parts Exchange

Many parts of an e-bike can be replaced, with the most frequently replaced ones being:

  • Motor
  • Controler
  • Display
  • Throttle
  • Pedal Sensor

Batteries

The battery of an e-bike is crucial as it stores the energy needed to power the motor. It is likely the most significant component of an e-bike, alongside the motor. The battery can be installed either on the frame or on the rack.
