About Us

Our small business began operating in 2019, during the COVID 19 pandemic when many well-known bike shops stopped repairing e-bikes. We specialize in repairing all types of e-bikes and ensure that our expert mechanics are always trained on the latest developments, providing you with the highest attention to detail.
